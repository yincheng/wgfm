About Us

World Gym Fort McMurray

The iconic World Gym brand was founded in 1976 by Joe Gold during the glory days of “Muscle Beach” in Venice, California. Since that Time World Gym has evolved into an internationally recognized brand that has captured the imagination of people around the globe.

That imagination spoke to local resident Peter Allen who brought the World Gym experience to Fort McMurray. Since our grand opening in September 2010, we have now developed into Fort McMurray’s premium health and fitness club destination.

The success of our gym has only been possible due to our staff, but most importantly, you, our loyal members.

Thank you Fort McMurray!
