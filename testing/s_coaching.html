>> Andrew Bambury

Andrew is well recognized in the fitness industry as an athlete, personal trainer and photographer. His passion started at a young age as he played on just about every sports team in school. At the age of 16 Andrew began working out and by the age of 20 he was competing in his first bodybuilding show. From there he completed two degrees: Bachelor of Kinesiology and a Bachelor of Physical Education. He obtained his strength and conditioning specialist certification (CSCS) and has competed in 7 muscle and fitness shows to date. In 2009 Andrew started his company called Body By Design Fitness. He has worked with hundreds of people achieve their fitness goals and practices what he preaches.
